SUNLIFE OFFERS

Finance Options

Buy the system outright, or own for $0 down and make payments lower than your old utility payment!

Detailed Service

We’ll send our solar study specialists over to assess your roof and shading so we can make sure your solar needs are custom tailored to your home.

Free Consultation

First we need to know how much you’re paying for electricity each month. The more you’re spending, the more you’ll save. If you’re paying more than an average of $125 a month on your energy bill, we can help offset most or all of your energy needs with solar!

Design & Installation

We draw up a plan tailored to the specifications of your home, have you approve it, apply for local building permits, and then our skilled installation team will do the heavy lifting and install your new solar system.

Customer Service

From the moment you say go, we provide you unparalleled customer service before, during, and after your solar experience with us.
